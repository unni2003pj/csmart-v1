In the ever-evolving landscape of the Satellite industry, staying ahead requires cutting-edge solutions. Welcome to a world where innovation meets excellence, where satellite providers find their perfect partner in seamless operations. Our Csmart Satellite Digital Service Enablement Platform (DSEP) is your comprehensive solution, catering to the unique demands of the Satellite industry, ensuring efficiency, adaptability, and unmatched service quality.

For years, satellite communications have been infrastructure-based, with satellite operators firmly controlling both space and ground segments. Yet, the winds of change are blowing. New technologies and business models are ushering in a new era, giving rise to Virtual Satellite Network Operators (VNOs) and Enablers (VNEs), reshaping the industry.

Satellite VNO

In a world where owning physical network assets is no longer a necessity, Satellite VNOs emerge as the agile players. By leasing satellite capacity on a wholesale basis, they offer business-focused connectivity services, from broadband to IoT. Csmart Satellite DSEP lower barriers to entry, flexible business models, and customized services tailored to specific industry needs make VNOs the driving force in underserved markets.

Satellite VNE

Behind every successful Satellite VNO stands a Satellite VNE. For Satellite Virtual Network Enablers (VNEs), Csmart Satellite DSEP is the linchpin for seamless operations. Platform not only streamlines wholesale billing with precision but also empowers VNEs to perform bulk provisioning effortlessly. Furthermore, it simplifies the complex task of reconciliation, ensuring that operations run smoothly and efficiently, allowing VNEs to focus on driving innovation and business growth.

Csmart Satellite CRM

CRM empowers to create a unified source of truth for customer information, design personalized service plans, and keep track of all interactions across various touchpoints.
